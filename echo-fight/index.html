GAME INFO

Clone Me is a fast-paced combat game where you battle your own past actions.

Every move you make is recorded, and after a short delay, a clone appears and replays those exact actions. As time passes, more clones spawn — turning your own strategy into your biggest challenge.

Algorithms Used

  • Time-Stamped Recording: Captures every player action with exact timing.
  • Replay / Ghost Playback: Clones mimic past actions exactly as recorded.
  • Finite State Machine (FSM): Controls states like Idle, Move, Jump, Attack.
  • Collision Detection: Handles hits, damage, and interactions.
  • Delay-Based Spawn: Spawns clones after a fixed time delay.
  • Circular Buffer: Stores recent actions efficiently.

What Problem It Solves

Most combat games rely on predictable enemies or simple AI, which become repetitive and easy to master. Clone Me solves this by:

  • Turning the player’s own skill and behavior into the challenge.
  • Creating infinite, unpredictable difficulty without complex AI.
  • Forcing players to adapt, improve, and rethink their strategy constantly.
